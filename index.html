Biography

Yi Wang is currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute of Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Systems, School of Earth and Space Sciences, Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Yu Liu, Prof. Fan Zhang, and Prof. Shanjun Mao. During his doctoral studies, he visited the Centre for Frontier AI Research at the Agency for Science, Technology and Research in Singapore, collaborating with Prof. Ivor W. Tsang and Dr. Jing Li. His primary research interests include Geo-Spatial Artificial Intelligence, Spatio-Temporal Data Mining, and Social Sensing.

Yi Wang has served as the Principal Investigator for the National Natural Science Foundation of China Youth Student Basic Research Project (Ph.D. Student). He has been honored with the National Scholarship, Leo KoGuan Scholarship, and Lin Chao Geography Scholarship, among other awards. He was also selected as a "Rising Star in GIS" (Rank 1) at the College GIS Forums of China. His research has been published in prestigious international journals in the fields of geographic information systems and artificial intelligence, including IEEE TPAMI, ISPRS JPRS, IEEE TITS, IEEE TGRS, and Information Fusion. In terms of academic service, he has served as a Program Committee Member for ACM SIGSPATIAL conferences and has provided peer review services for over 20 renowned international journals and conferences in the fields of geography and spatiotemporal modeling, including IEEE TPAMI, ISPRS JPRS, TR Part E, IJGIS, IEEE TAI, and PR.
